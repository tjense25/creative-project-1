Puerto Rico Needs Our Help

Hurricane Maria, the strongest hurricane to make landfall in the US in nearly 100 years, has devastated the island of Puerto Rico. 155 MPH winds and non-stop rainfall has caused massive destruction, cut the entire island off of power, and flooded thousands of homes and businesses. Rebuilding the island in the aftermath of this terrible disaster will be one of the most arduous humanitarian projects of the century.
